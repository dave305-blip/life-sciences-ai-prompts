# Life Sciences AI Prompt Library

A curated collection of practical AI prompts and workflows for pharmaceutical field teams.

This repository focuses on real operational use cases for:

- Pharma sales representatives
- Medical science liaisons (MSLs)
- Medical affairs teams
- KOL engagement teams
- Field commercial operations

The prompts and workflows are derived from the editorial resource:

**Life Sciences AI Playbook**

https://lifesciencesaiplaybook.com

## Governance Note

These prompts are intended for experimentation and workflow exploration.

Teams operating in regulated Life Sciences environments should avoid entering sensitive or confidential information into consumer AI tools unless explicitly approved by organizational policy.

Many organizations evaluate governed enterprise tools or domain-specific platforms designed for Life Sciences workflows.
